About Alviolis

At Alviolis, we are committed to providing clothing that combines timeless style with modern comfort. Our garments are meticulously crafted to ensure superior quality and a perfect fit.

1

Premium Polycotton

Our fabric is a premium blend of polycotton, offering both durability and a soft feel.

2

240 GSM Pique Knit

We use a substantial 240 GSM Pique Knit Fabric, which provides excellent structure and breathability.

3

Bio Wash & Lasting Color

Our clothes undergo a special bio wash treatment and are made with long-lasting color to ensure they stay vibrant wash after wash.

4

Regular Fit & Unisex Sizing

All of our garments are designed with a Regular Fit and come in a wide range of Unisex Sizes.
